# Comprehensive Job Descriptions by Industry

Complete, robust job descriptions for all critical roles across 8 industries at all growth stages. Each description includes responsibilities, qualifications, required skills, and KPIs.

## Executive Level (All Industries)

### Chief Executive Officer (CEO)
**Reports To:** Board of Directors  
**Typical Salary Range:** $200K - $500K+  
**Growth Stage:** All stages (role adapts with scale)

**Full Responsibilities:**
- Develop and execute comprehensive business strategy aligned with company vision
- Establish organizational culture, values, and performance standards
- Set revenue targets, growth objectives, and strategic milestones for all departments
- Lead capital allocation decisions and budget prioritization
- Oversee all C-suite executives and their direct reports
- Represent company to investors, board, customers, and stakeholders
- Drive market positioning, competitive differentiation, and brand strategy
- Make final decisions on major hires, organizational restructuring, and strategic initiatives
- Ensure regulatory compliance and risk management across all business functions
- Quarterly earnings reports and board presentations
- Build and maintain investor relationships and capital raise strategy
- Accountability for P&L, EBITDA targets, and shareholder returns

**Required Qualifications:**
- 10+ years progressive leadership experience (5+ in industry)
- Track record of scaling company from startup to $10M+ revenue
- MBA or equivalent business graduate degree preferred
- Demonstrated ability to build and lead executive teams
- Experience raising capital from institutional investors
- Proficiency in financial analysis and strategic planning
- Excellent public speaking and communication skills

**Required Skills:**
- Strategic vision and long-term planning
- Financial acumen and P&L management
- Executive leadership and team building
- Investor and board management
- Crisis management and decision-making under uncertainty
- Sales and business development
- Change management and organizational transformation
- Data-driven decision making

**Key Performance Indicators:**
- Revenue growth rate (target: 30-50% YoY)
- Gross margin achievement vs. target
- EBITDA margin (target: 15-25% by year 3)
- Customer acquisition cost vs. lifetime value ratio
- Employee retention rate (target: 90%+)
- Time to close board meetings and strategic decisions
- Investor satisfaction scores
- Market share growth in primary vertical

### Chief Financial Officer (CFO)
**Reports To:** CEO  
**Typical Salary Range:** $150K - $400K+  
**Growth Stage:** Stage 2+ (once company reaches $1M+ revenue)

**Full Responsibilities:**
- Develop and manage overall financial strategy aligned with business objectives
- Oversee accounting, financial reporting, and audit functions
- Implement financial controls and internal audit procedures
- Manage capital structure, debt instruments, and financing strategy
- Conduct financial due diligence for M&A opportunities
- Develop annual budgets and 5-year financial forecasts
- Manage cash flow, working capital, and treasury operations
- Oversee tax planning and compliance (federal, state, international if applicable)
- Lead quarterly and annual financial close processes
- Prepare materials for investor meetings, board presentations, and earnings calls
- Implement financial systems and ERP solutions
- Supervise all accounting, finance, and controller staff
- Ensure SOX compliance and financial audit requirements
- Manage relationships with external auditors and legal counsel
- Develop metrics dashboards for executive decision-making

**Required Qualifications:**
- CPA or CFA required (exceptions with senior accounting background)
- 12+ years accounting/finance experience (8+ in operations/corporate finance)
- 5+ years experience in fast-growing or scaling company environment
- Demonstrated experience with venture capital or institutional investor management
- Expertise with GAAP financial reporting and Sarbanes-Oxley compliance
- Experience with M&A transactions and integration
- Proficiency with financial systems (SAP, NetSuite, Workday, etc.)

**Required Skills:**
- Financial statement analysis and reporting
- FP&A (forecasting, budgeting, scenario modeling)
- Cash flow management
- Cost accounting and profitability analysis
- Internal controls and compliance
- Capital structure optimization
- Investor and board reporting
- Excel and financial modeling
- ERP systems management
- Tax strategy planning

**Key Performance Indicators:**
- Accuracy of financial forecasts vs. actuals (target: 95%+)
- Monthly close cycle time (target: 5 days)
- Financial audit findings (target: zero material findings)
- Cash flow variance vs. budget (target: <5%)
- Headcount to finance staff ratio (target: 50:1)
- Cost of capital reduction (year over year)
- Tax rate optimization (achieving lowest legal rate)
- Balance sheet efficiency metrics (receivables DSO, payables DPO)
