# Minimum Staffing Requirements by Industry and Growth Stage

Comprehensive staffing models ensuring every company has critical roles staffed from inception. Each company must have mandatory: Marketing, Legal, HR, and Full Executive (CEO, COO, CFO).

## MANDATORY BASELINE (ALL COMPANIES, ALL INDUSTRIES)

### Executive Level (3 Minimum)
1. **Chief Executive Officer (CEO)** - CRITICAL
   - Full Name: Auto-populated from company founder/selected person
   - Title: Chief Executive Officer
   - Reports To: Board of Directors (if exists) or ownership
   - Full-Time Required: Yes
   - Minimum Experience: 5+ years in industry
   - Specialty: Business Strategy, Operations, Sales, Leadership
   - Salary Range: $150K-$500K+ (depends on stage and revenue)
   - Voice: Professional, confident, authoritative
   - Start Date: Company formation date

2. **Chief Operations Officer (COO) / VP Operations** - CRITICAL
   - Full Name: Needs hiring or internal promotion
   - Title: Chief Operations Officer
   - Reports To: CEO
   - Full-Time Required: Yes
   - Minimum Experience: 8+ years operations, 3+ years management
   - Specialty: Operations, Process Management, Scaling
   - Salary Range: $120K-$350K+ (depends on stage)
   - Voice: Direct, organized, solution-focused
   - Start Date: Recommend by month 1 of operations

3. **Chief Financial Officer (CFO)** - CRITICAL
   - Full Name: Needs hiring or internal promotion
   - Title: Chief Financial Officer
   - Reports To: CEO
   - Full-Time Required: Yes
   - Minimum Experience: 10+ years finance, 5+ years CFO-equivalent
   - Specialty: Financial Management, Accounting, Capital Planning
   - Salary Range: $120K-$400K+ (depends on stage)
   - Voice: Detail-oriented, analytical, measured
   - Start Date: Recommend by month 2-3 of operations

### Department Heads (3 Minimum)

4. **Head of Marketing** - MANDATORY
   - Full Name: Needs hiring or internal promotion
   - Title: Head of Marketing / VP Marketing
   - Reports To: CEO
   - Full-Time Required: Yes (can be part-time initially for <10 people)
   - Minimum Experience: 5+ years marketing, 3+ years in industry
   - Specialty: Digital Marketing, Brand, Lead Generation, Go-to-Market
   - Salary Range: $60K-$150K+ (depends on stage)
   - Voice: Creative, energetic, persuasive
   - Start Date: Recommend by month 1 of operations
   - Key Responsibilities:
     - Brand positioning and company positioning
     - Lead generation and customer acquisition
     - Website and digital presence
     - Marketing campaigns and promotions
     - Analytics and ROI measurement
     - Content strategy (blogs, videos, social media)
     - Sales enablement and marketing collateral

5. **General Counsel / Legal Lead** - MANDATORY
   - Full Name: Needs hiring or internal promotion
   - Title: General Counsel / VP Legal
   - Reports To: CEO
   - Full-Time Required: Part-time or fractional initially, Full-time by Stage 2
   - Minimum Experience: 5+ years legal, 3+ years in-house counsel
   - Specialty: Contract Law, Compliance, Risk Management
   - Salary Range: $70K-$200K+ (depends on stage)
   - Voice: Cautious, precise, protective
   - Start Date: Recommend month 1 of operations (part-time initially)
   - Key Responsibilities:
     - Entity formation and governance
     - Contract review and negotiation
     - Compliance (regulatory, industry-specific)
     - IP protection and management
     - HR/Employment law
     - Risk management and insurance
     - Vendor management and agreements
     - Dispute resolution

6. **Head of Human Resources** - MANDATORY
   - Full Name: Needs hiring or internal promotion
   - Title: Head of HR / VP People
   - Reports To: CEO or COO
   - Full-Time Required: Part-time initially, Full-time by 20 employees
   - Minimum Experience: 5+ years HR, 2+ years management
   - Specialty: Recruitment, People Development, Compliance
   - Salary Range: $55K-$150K+ (depends on stage and complexity)
   - Voice: Supportive, empathetic, organized
   - Start Date: Recommend month 1-2 of operations (part-time initially)
   - Key Responsibilities:
     - Recruitment and hiring
     - Benefits administration (health insurance, 401k)
     - Employee onboarding and orientation
     - Performance management and reviews
     - Payroll and compensation
     - Culture and engagement
     - Compliance (labor laws, discrimination, wage/hour)
     - Training and development

## GROWTH STAGE 1: STARTUP (1-10 EMPLOYEES)

### Staffing Model
- **Total Headcount:** 1-10
- **Full-Time Employees:** 5-8
- **Part-Time/Contract:** 2-5 (including fractional executives)
- **Executive Structure:** CEO (founder), fractional CFO, fractional COO
- **Recommended Model:** Founder as CEO, hire fractional finance/legal/HR initially

### Required Executive Leadership
1. **CEO/Founder** - Must be present
   - Often wears multiple hats
   - Key roles: Sales, Strategy, Culture, Fundraising

2. **CFO (Fractional/Part-Time)** - Highly recommended
   - 10 hours/week initially, can share with other founders
   - Can be CFO service provider or fractional executive
   - Focus: Cash management, accounting, tax compliance

3. **COO/Operations Lead** - Can be founder initially
   - If not founder, hire by month 3
   - Focus: Operations, processes, scaling

### Required Department Heads
1. **Head of Marketing** (Full or part-time, 20-40 hours/week)
   - Focus: Product-market fit, customer acquisition
   - Can be founder initially (if founder has marketing background)
   - Recommend external hire or agency support

2. **Legal (Fractional Counsel)** (5-10 hours/week)
   - Can use legal services firm or fractional counsel
   - Focus: Entity formation, basic contracts, compliance

3. **Head of HR (Part-Time Advisor)** (5-10 hours/week initially)
   - Can use PEO service or HR consultant
   - Focus: Hiring, basic policies, payroll setup

### Staffing Template - Stage 1
```
EXECUTIVE (3 people minimum - includes fractional)
├── CEO/Founder (Full-Time) [Founder or Hired]
│   ├── Responsibilities: Overall strategy, sales, fundraising, culture
│   ├── Salary: $100K-200K or sweat equity
│   └── Voice: Visionary, energetic, confident
│
├── CFO/Finance (Part-Time/Fractional) [Fractional service or hire]
│   ├── Responsibilities: Accounting, tax, cash management
│   ├── Hours: 10-15/week
│   ├── Salary: $3K-$8K/month
│   └── Voice: Organized, analytical, cautious
│
└── Operations Lead (Full-Time or part-time) [Founder or Hire]
    ├── Responsibilities: Day-to-day operations, processes
    ├── Salary: $60K-$100K
    └── Voice: Practical, efficient, detail-oriented

DEPARTMENT HEADS (3 minimum)
├── Head of Marketing (Full or Part-Time) [Founder if capable, else hire]
│   ├── Responsibilities: Brand, lead gen, customer acquisition
│   ├── Hours: 30-40/week
│   ├── Salary: $50K-$80K or contract/agency
│   └── Voice: Creative, energetic, strategic
│
├── General Counsel (Fractional) [Legal service firm or lawyer]
│   ├── Responsibilities: Contracts, compliance, IP
│   ├── Hours: 5-10/week
│   ├── Salary: $2K-$5K/month or per-project
│   └── Voice: Precise, cautious, protective
│
└── Head of HR (Part-Time Advisor) [PEO or HR consultant]
    ├── Responsibilities: Hiring, onboarding, payroll, basic policies
    ├── Hours: 5-10/week
    ├── Salary: $1K-$3K/month
    └── Voice: Supportive, organized, encouraging

SUPPORT STAFF (2-4 people)
├── Sales/Business Development (Full-Time)
├── Product/Engineering (Full-Time) - If tech company
├── Administrative/Operations Support (Full-Time or Part-Time)
└── Customer Service/Support (Full-Time or Part-Time)
```

- **Part-Time/Contract:** 1-5

## GROWTH STAGE 4: MATURITY (76-150 EMPLOYEES)

### Staffing Model
- **Total Headcount:** 76-150
- **Full-Time Employees:** 70-145
- **Executive Structure:** Full mature C-suite with specialized VP/Director layer
- **Department Model:** Well-established departments with sub-teams

### Required Executive Leadership (All Full-Time)
1. **CEO** - Full-Time
2. **CFO** - Full-Time
3. **COO** - Full-Time
4. **VP Sales** - Full-Time
5. **VP Product** (if applicable) - Full-Time
6. **VP Marketing** - Full-Time
7. **Chief Compliance Officer** or **VP Compliance** - Full-Time (new for Stage 4)
8. **VP/Director of Business Development** - Full-Time (new for Stage 4)

### Required Department Heads (All Full-Time)
1. **General Counsel** - Full-Time
2. **Chief Human Resources Officer** or **VP People** - Full-Time
3. **Head of Customer Success** - Full-Time
4. **VP/Director of Operations** - Full-Time (dedicated role)
5. **Controller/Finance Manager** - Full-Time
6. **Director of Quality/Compliance** (if regulated) - Full-Time

### Additional Roles
- Multiple sales managers overseeing teams
- Multiple customer success managers and team
- Marketing specialists in various disciplines (digital, content, events)
- Multiple finance staff (accounting, analysis, planning)
- Expanded HR team (recruiting, payroll, benefits, culture)
- Compliance and quality specialists
- Regional or vertical sales managers

### Industry-Specific Requirements at This Stage
- Real Estate: Regional managers, transaction teams, training
- Tech: Engineering manager(s), product specialists, DevOps/Infrastructure
- Finance: Compliance team, risk management, audit
- Healthcare: Clinical team expansion, HIPAA officer
- Legal: Multiple practice group leads, paralegals
- Manufacturing: Multiple plant managers, quality engineers

## CRITICAL SUCCESS FACTORS

✅ **Never launch without these roles:**
- CEO (founder or hired)
- CFO (full-time or fractional)
- Head of Marketing
- General Counsel (in-house or retained)
- Head of HR (dedicated or shared/part-time)

✅ **Scale as you grow:**
- Stage 1: Keep costs low, use fractional resources
- Stage 2: Transition to full-time critical roles
- Stage 3: Add specialized department managers
- Stage 4: Mature leadership, specialized expertise
- Stage 5: Large, specialized org chart by industry

✅ **Maintain reporting structure:**
- CEO reports to board
- C-suite (CFO, COO, VP Sales, etc.) report to CEO
- Department heads report to CEO or C-suite
- Individual contributors report to managers

✅ **Use Job Descriptions for Hiring:**
- Templates in JOB_DESCRIPTIONS_BY_INDUSTRY.md
- Admin Agent customizes for your specific needs
- Ensures quality and completeness
- Provides benchmarking for compensation
